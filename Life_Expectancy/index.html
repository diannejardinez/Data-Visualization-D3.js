MIND THE GAP

Life Expectancy

Gapminder Visualization

This visualization shows how life expectancy has been
increasing over the years with GDP per capita as referenced by Han Rosling.
This video from BBC - 'The Joy of Stats' shows a live display of the graph below with Han Rosling.

Select "play" or "pause" to start or stop the visualization.
The dropdown menu allows for any filtering of the countries in the dataset.
Click on any circle to view the Country, Continent, Life Expectancy, GDP Per Capita, and Population of that datapoint.

For more information about this data visit Gapminder webpage. Note dataset is from 1800-2014.
